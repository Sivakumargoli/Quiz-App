<h2>Description:</h2>

<p>  I have successfully developed a text-based quiz application using Python and the Kivy framework to meet the requirements of our client, Bharat Intern. The application is designed to provide an engaging and interactive quiz experience for users, allowing them to test their knowledge on various topics.

Key Features:
User-Friendly Interface: The app features an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making it easy for users to navigate through the quizzes and answer questions.

Quiz Categories: Users can choose from a variety of quiz categories, ensuring that the app caters to a broad range of interests and knowledge areas.

Randomized Questions: Questions are presented in a random order, providing a unique quiz experience each time.

Scoring and Leaderboard: The app keeps track of user scores and displays a leaderboard, fostering competition and motivation to improve.

Feedback and Explanation: Users receive immediate feedback on their quiz performance, and correct answers are explained, enhancing the educational aspect of the app.

Progress Tracking: Users can track their progress and review their quiz history to monitor their improvement.

Technology Stack:

Programming Language: Python
Framework: Kivy
